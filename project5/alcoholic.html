Alcoholic Drink Recipes

Corpse Reviver

Ingredients:

  • Absinthe, to rinse
  • 3/4 ounce London dry gin
  • 3/4 ounce Lillet blanc
  • 3/4 ounce orange liqueur
  • 3/4 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Steps:

  1. Rinse the inside of a chilled coupe or cocktail glass with absinthe, discard the excess and set the glass aside.
  2. Add the gin, Lillet blanc, orange liqueur, and lemon juice into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into the prepared glass.

Tools:

- None needed.

Dirty Martini

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ ounces gin or vodka
  • 1/2 ounce dry vermouth
  • 1/2 ounce olive brine
  • Garnish: 2 to 4 olives

Steps:

  1. Add the gin or vodka, vermouth, and olive brine to a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake for 15–20 seconds until well chilled.
  3. Double strain through a fine mesh strainer into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with a skewer of olives.

Tools:

- None needed.

Paper Plane

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 ounce bourbon
  • 3/4 ounce Aperol
  • 3/4 ounce Amaro Nonino Quintessential
  • 3/4 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed

Steps:

  1. Add the bourbon, Aperol, Amaro Nonino, and lemon juice into a shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a coupe glass.

Tools:

- None needed.
