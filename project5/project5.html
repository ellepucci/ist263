About the Author

Welcome to the ultimate recipebook!

My name is Elle and I am the author of this cookbook. Having a list of go-to drinks is key for an easy lifestyle. Drinks are so much fun to play around with and I hope you enjoy the ones I have created for you. Some of my absolute favorites are listed on page two of the cookbook. If alcoholic drinks are not your thing or you are not 21 years of age, refer to page three for several choices of mocktails.
